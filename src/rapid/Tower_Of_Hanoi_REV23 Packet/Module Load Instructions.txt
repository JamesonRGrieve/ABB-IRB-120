To load these modules to a RobotStudio virtual controller
1. Open the RAPID tab.
2. Locate T_ROB1 on the left side of the screen (120-501650_Turvey, TAPID, T_ROB1)
3. Right Click T_ROB1
4. Select Load Module
5. Navigate to your desired module folder.
6. Highlight all modules you wish to load
	NOTE if upgrading to latest revision load all modules in this folder.
7. Press Open
8. Say YES to all notification stating a module with the same name already exists in the task. 
9. Open your virtual flex pendant.
10. ensure motors are on.
11. press PP to Main in the production window
12. Press play and explore the new interface, programs, and features of the latest edition.
